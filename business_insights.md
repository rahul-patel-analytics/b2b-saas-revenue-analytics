## Funnel Performance

The overall Lead-to-Deal conversion stands at approximately 48%, indicating that nearly half of generated leads successfully move into pipeline.

The Deal-to-Win rate is approximately 32.75%, suggesting moderate closing efficiency.

## Sales Representative Analysis

## Acquisition Channel Effectiveness

LinkedIn emerges as the strongest acquisition channel, delivering both:

- Highest Lead-to-Win conversion
- Highest revenue contribution

This indicates strong alignment between targeting strategy and ideal customer profile (ICP).


## Sales Cycle Velocity

The average sales cycle for won deals is ~18 days.

This indicates a fast-moving SMB SaaS model rather than a long enterprise cycle, aligning with observed deal values and win rates.


## Strategic Considerations

1. Investigate deal-stage losses to improve closing efficiency.
2. Increase investment in high-performing channels such as LinkedIn.
3. Explore deal-size segmentation strategies across reps.
4. Monitor sales cycle changes as deal value scales.
